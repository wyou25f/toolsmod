**Tools+** is a high-performance, ultra-lightweight, and server-side friendly utility designed to shatter the hardcoded vanilla boundaries of Minecraft. It grants server administrators, mapmakers, and players absolute power over item modification through a seamless, robust, and optimized ecosystem without adding heavy or game-breaking dependencies.

## English 🇬🇧

### 👥 Detailed Audience Breakdown
* 🛡️ **Server Administrators:** Take full control of your economy and reward systems. Easily forge unique event rewards, custom high-tier donor gear, or devastating custom drops for your mythical bosses without needing complex RPG plugins.
* 🎬 **Content Creators & Mapmakers:** Streamline your workflow. Gear up instantly for mod showcases, perform rapid combat testing, or create overpowered custom legendary items directly embedded into your RPG adventure maps.
* ⚔️ **Survival & Sandbox Lovers:** Want to stir up some absolute chaos with your friends on a private server? Push your gear past the safety limits and discover what `Efficiency X` or `Sharpness X` can actually do to the game world!

### ✨ Deep-Dive Features
* ⚡ **Absolute Limit Bypass:** Forget standard anvil restrictions. Forcefully enchant any item in your main hand up to level 255 (`Sharpness X`, `Fortune X`, `Knockback X`) instantly. The system completely bypasses vanilla caps safely.
* ⚙️ **Pure Server-Side Architecture:** Zero client-side requirement! Players can connect using a completely vanilla Minecraft client. The mod introduces no extra network bloat, ensuring maximum tick-rate performance (20 TPS) and zero lag spikes.
* 🌐 **Dynamic On-The-Fly Localization:** Includes fully native multi-language translation out of the box. No server restarts or config reloads needed—change the interface system language instantly with immediate visual feedback.
* 🛠️ **Rock-Solid Stability:** Written in optimized, modern code. Safely modifies item NBT data without risking world corruption or breaking interactions with other multiplayer optimization mods.

## 🚀 Quick Start / Быстрый старт

1. 📥 Download **Tools+** for your preferred loader (**Fabric** or **NeoForge**).
2. 📂 Drop the `.jar` file into your server's `mods` folder.
3. 🎮 Start the server, grab any item in your hand, and execute the command!

1. 📥 Скачайте **Tools+** для вашего загрузчика (**Fabric** или **NeoForge**).
2. 📂 Поместите файл `.jar` в папку `mods` вашего сервера.
3. 🎮 Запустите сервер, возьмите любой предмет в руку и введите команду!

## 📜 Commands & Syntax / Синтаксис команд

| Command / Команда | Arguments / Аргументы | Description (EN) | Описание (RU) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 🪓 `/tools enchant` | `[enchantment_id] [level]` | Enchants the item held in your main hand up to level 255. | Зачаровывает предмет в главной руке до 255 уровня включительно. |
| 🌐 `/tools lang` | `[en/ru]` | Instantly updates the display language for all mod systems. | Моментально изменяет язык вывода сообщений мода. |

> 💡 **Examples / Примеры:** 
> * `/tools enchant sharpness 10` — *Creates an ultimate weapon / Создает ультимативное оружие*
> * `/tools lang ru` — *Switches interface to Russian / Переключает интерфейс на русский язык*
